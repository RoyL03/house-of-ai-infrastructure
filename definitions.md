Definitions: AI Infrastructure for Women-Led Businesses
AI Employee

An AI employee is a digital system assigned a defined role inside a business.
It performs specific tasks, follows operational rules, and produces outputs such as research summaries, marketing drafts, or customer responses.

AI Workforce

An AI workforce is a collection of AI employees performing operational roles within a company.

Examples include:

research AI

marketing AI

analytics AI

customer communication AI

Together these systems function as a digital support team for the founder.

AI Infrastructure

AI infrastructure is the system of connected tools, workflows, and knowledge libraries that allow artificial intelligence to operate continuously inside a business.

Instead of relying entirely on human labor, AI infrastructure enables many operational tasks to run in the background, supporting the founder.
