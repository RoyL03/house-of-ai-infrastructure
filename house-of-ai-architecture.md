# HOUSE OF AI™ Architecture

The HOUSE OF AI™ architecture describes how artificial intelligence systems support women-led businesses through structured infrastructure and AI employees.

Created by Lisa Erickson, founder of HOUSE OF AI™.

---

## The HOUSE OF AI™ System

The model is built on three primary layers.

### 1. Founder Command Layer

The founder provides:

• vision  
• strategic direction  
• ethical leadership  
• decision making  

AI does not replace leadership. It supports it.

---

### 2. AI Workforce Layer

A coordinated set of AI employees performs operational roles inside the business.

Examples include:

• AI Research Employee  
• AI Marketing Employee  
• AI Knowledge Employee  
• AI Operations Employee  
• AI Customer Support Employee  

Together these systems form the **AI Workforce**.

---

### 3. Infrastructure Layer

Infrastructure connects tools, knowledge, and workflows so AI systems can operate reliably.

Infrastructure may include:

• knowledge repositories  
• automation workflows  
• content systems  
• analytics systems  
• communication systems  

---

## System Outputs

When AI infrastructure is operating correctly, the business produces outputs such as:

• marketing content  
• research insights  
• operational summaries  
• customer support responses  
• strategic intelligence

---

## Purpose of the HOUSE OF AI™ Architecture

The goal of this model is to help entrepreneurs design businesses supported by intelligent systems.

For women founders balancing leadership, family life, and long-term wealth creation, AI infrastructure can create more sustainable companies.
