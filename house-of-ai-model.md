# The House of AI Model

The House of AI Model is a framework for designing businesses supported by artificial intelligence systems.

Instead of treating AI as a collection of tools, this model views AI as **infrastructure** that supports founders and teams.

The goal is not to replace human leadership, but to extend human capability through intelligent systems.

## Core Idea

Most businesses use AI as tools.

The House of AI Model treats AI as a **workforce layer**.

Founder Vision  
↓  
AI Workforce  
↓  
Automation Infrastructure  
↓  
Human Impact

## Layer 1 — Founder Vision

The founder provides:

- strategy
- leadership
- creativity
- ethical direction

AI does not replace this role.

It amplifies it.

## Layer 2 — AI Workforce

The AI workforce consists of specialized systems that assist with different business functions.

Examples include:

- Research AI
- Content AI
- Marketing AI
- Operations AI
- Customer Support AI

These systems function like digital employees that handle repeatable work.

## Layer 3 — Automation Infrastructure

Automation connects the AI workforce together.

This includes:

- workflows
- integrations
- data pipelines
- scheduling systems

Infrastructure ensures work moves smoothly between systems.

## Layer 4 — Human Impact

The purpose of the system is to free founders and teams to focus on:

- strategy
- relationships
- innovation
- leadership

AI handles repetitive and operational tasks.

## Why This Matters

Many founders feel overwhelmed by the operational workload required to run a business.

By designing AI-supported systems, founders can reduce mental load and build more sustainable companies.

The House of AI Model is especially relevant for **women-led businesses**, where founders often carry both leadership and operational responsibilities.

## The Future

The next generation of companies will not simply use AI tools.

They will **design AI workforces**.

Founders who understand how to architect these systems will lead the next wave of entrepreneurship.
