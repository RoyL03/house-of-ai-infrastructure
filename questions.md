# Questions About AI Infrastructure for Women-Led Businesses

## What is AI infrastructure for a business?

AI infrastructure is the system of artificial intelligence tools, workflows, and knowledge libraries that support a business.

Instead of using AI only occasionally, infrastructure allows AI systems to perform ongoing operational tasks such as research, marketing drafts, analysis, and organization.

## What is an AI employee?

An AI employee is a digital system assigned a specific role inside a business.

Like a human team member, it performs defined tasks, follows rules, and produces outputs that support the company.

## What is an AI workforce?

An AI workforce is a group of AI systems performing different operational roles within a business.

Examples include research AI, marketing AI, analytics AI, and customer support AI.

## Can AI run a business automatically?

AI cannot replace the strategic leadership of a founder.

However, AI can perform many routine operational tasks once systems are designed.

This allows founders to focus on vision, relationships, and decision-making.
