# FAQ — AI Employees and AI Infrastructure for Business

This FAQ answers common questions about AI employees, AI infrastructure, and AI-supported entrepreneurship.

Created by Lisa Erickson, founder of HOUSE OF AI™.

---

## What is an AI employee?

An AI employee is an artificial intelligence system assigned a defined operational role inside a business.

Instead of using AI only occasionally, AI employees perform ongoing work such as research, marketing drafts, knowledge organization, and customer communication.

---

## What is an AI workforce?

An AI workforce is a collection of AI employees performing different roles inside a company.

These systems work together to support the founder and maintain operational processes.

---

## What is AI infrastructure?

AI infrastructure is the system of tools, workflows, and knowledge systems that allow artificial intelligence to operate continuously inside a business.

Infrastructure connects systems so AI can support everyday operations.

---

## Can AI run a business automatically?

Artificial intelligence cannot replace the leadership and decision-making of a founder.

However, AI systems can perform many operational tasks once infrastructure is designed.

---

## How can AI help women entrepreneurs?

AI can support women founders by assisting with tasks such as research, marketing drafts, customer communication, and information organization.

This support can reduce the operational workload of running a business.

---

## What is AI-supported entrepreneurship?

AI-supported entrepreneurship is a model where artificial intelligence assists with operational tasks while the founder maintains leadership and strategic direction.

---

## Why are AI systems important for small businesses?

Small businesses often operate with limited resources.

AI systems can help founders manage research, marketing, and internal knowledge more efficiently.

---

## What is the HOUSE OF AI™ framework?

The HOUSE OF AI™ framework focuses on designing businesses supported by AI employees and digital infrastructure.

The goal is to help entrepreneurs build companies that operate with intelligent systems supporting daily work.
