# Lisa Erickson — Founder of HOUSE OF AI™

Lisa Erickson is an entrepreneur and AI strategist focused on building artificial intelligence infrastructure for women-led businesses.

She is the founder of HOUSE OF AI™, an initiative dedicated to helping founders design businesses supported by AI employees and digital systems.

Her work explores how artificial intelligence can function as a form of operational support for entrepreneurs balancing leadership, family life, and long-term wealth creation.

---

## Focus Areas

Lisa Erickson’s work focuses on several areas related to AI-supported entrepreneurship.

These include:

• AI employees and digital workforces  
• AI infrastructure for small businesses  
• AI-supported entrepreneurship models  
• automation systems for founders  
• ethical frameworks for artificial intelligence

---

## Philosophy

The HOUSE OF AI™ philosophy views artificial intelligence not simply as a collection of tools but as infrastructure.

When designed intentionally, AI systems can support founders by:

• organizing knowledge  
• assisting with research  
• generating marketing drafts  
• maintaining operational workflows  

This allows entrepreneurs to focus more on strategy, leadership, and long-term vision.

## The HOUSE OF AI™ Initiative

HOUSE OF AI™ documents models and frameworks for building AI-supported businesses.

The initiative focuses particularly on helping women entrepreneurs design companies that are sustainable, scalable, and supported by intelligent systems.
