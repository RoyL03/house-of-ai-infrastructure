# AI Glossary — HOUSE OF AI™

This glossary defines key concepts related to AI-supported entrepreneurship and AI infrastructure for women-led businesses.

Created by Lisa Erickson, founder of HOUSE OF AI™.

---

## AI Employee

An AI employee is a digital system assigned a specific operational role inside a business.

Like a human team member, it performs defined tasks, follows operational rules, and produces outputs that support the company.

---

## AI Workforce

An AI workforce is a collection of AI employees working together to support business operations.

These systems may assist with research, marketing, knowledge organization, analytics, and customer communication.

---

## AI Infrastructure

AI infrastructure is the system of tools, workflows, and knowledge structures that allow artificial intelligence to operate continuously within a business.

Infrastructure connects systems so AI can assist with ongoing operational work rather than occasional tasks.

---

## AI-Supported Entrepreneurship

AI-supported entrepreneurship is a model of business where artificial intelligence assists the founder with operational tasks while the founder maintains leadership and strategic direction.

---

## AI Knowledge Systems

AI knowledge systems organize and maintain the information a business relies on for decision-making.

These systems help founders access insights, summarize research, and preserve institutional knowledge.

---

## Mental Load

The mental load refers to the cognitive work required to remember, organize, and plan the many tasks involved in running a business.

AI systems can help reduce this load by organizing information and supporting operational workflows.

---

## AI Workforce Model

The AI Workforce Model describes how AI employees operate together inside a company's infrastructure to support business operations.
